The Changes We Need

Most priorities of the government are misaligned with what the populace deserves. They expect the improvement of day-to-day living instead of potential threats to their own lives. The government's stance on prioritizing military strength over agriculture, education, and healthcare show that they want control and incentives. We as ambassadors of change feel compelled to connect with influential people in communities and governments worldwide. Together, we fight for sound change to help eradicate all facets of poverty in society. We create opportunities with public officials to voice our opinions about the laws, regulations, programs, and cultural norms that impact world poverty. We work to bring about justice and renewal in disadvantaged indigenous and local communities through these connections and sway.

About the Advocates

Nutritious food. Shelter. Clean water. A decent education. Access to health care. And a safe and secure environment. All fundamental for a life free from hunger and poverty. Yet millions of people cannot afford them.
